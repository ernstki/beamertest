\begin{frame}[fragile]{Mermaid diagram (PDF version)}

section, but shows up in the README.

\note{\includegraphics{img/mermaid.svg}}

The Makefile generates a PDF version that can is embedded into the next
slide with LaTeX's \texttt{\textbackslash{}includegraphics}. So
